For My Dearest Mom

They say a mother's love is the closest thing to God's love on Earth, and I believe it. Your love has been my constant guide, my warmest comfort, and my strongest foundation.

My Beautiful Mom,

I was just sitting here thinking about my life, and so many of my best memories have you right at the center of them. I don't tell you enough how much I truly love you and appreciate you.

Thank you for all the big things, the sacrifices you made, the lessons you taught, and the endless love you've given. But thank you also for the little things: your comforting voice, you believing in me even when I didn't believe in myself, and the way you make everything feel like home.

You are my first home, my safest place, and my biggest supporter. I am who I am because of you. I hope you know how incredible you are and how much you are loved.

With all my heart,

your kasuli,

THANDY
